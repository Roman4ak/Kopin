Who Are We?

Our Gift Box Division was established in early 2019 under the management of PT Kedaung Oriental Porcelain Industry, which is a worldwide porcelain and tableware company established in 1989.

Within our first year, the Gift Box Division grew rapidly and succeeded in producing millions of Rigid Boxes with Various Shapes and Designs. Many local & foreign businesses have entrusted us in manufacturing their packaging. We have clients from Indonesia, America, Canada & Australia.

We are fully committed for continue to grow and help every business manufacturer from various fields in providing packaging that can added value to their products.

Our vision is to become rigid box manufacturer of choice in Global Market
